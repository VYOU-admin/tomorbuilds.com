Privacy Policy

Word Ties: Connections — Last updated July 5, 2026

Word Ties: Connections is developed by TDLabs LLC. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game statistics (games played, wins, streaks, best times)
  • App preferences (dark mode, auto-complete, haptics)
  • Daily play counts for the free play limit

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob — serves advertisements and may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device Settings under Privacy → Apple Advertising.

Apple Game Center — used for leaderboards. Apple may collect your Game Center alias and score data. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
