Privacy Policy

Word Ties: Connections — Last updated July 5, 2026

Word Ties: Connections ("the App") is developed and operated by TDLabs. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect or store any personally identifiable information on our servers. All game data (stats, preferences, streak counts) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs.

The following third-party services used within the App may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center — used for leaderboards. Apple may collect your Game Center alias and score data. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using data collected by Google. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings under Privacy → Apple Advertising, or through the Google Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game statistics (games played, wins, streaks, best times)
  • App preferences (dark mode, auto-complete toggle, haptics)
  • Daily and timed play counts for the free play gate

This data is not transmitted to our servers and is not shared with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tomor.builds@outlook.com.
